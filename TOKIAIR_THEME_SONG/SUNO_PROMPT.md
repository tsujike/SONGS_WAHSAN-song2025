## スタイル指定プロンプト

**音楽スタイル：**
- Upbeat, adventurous, energetic
- Japanese pop-rock or J-pop style
- Inspirational and uplifting
- Catchy melody with memorable chorus
- Modern production with acoustic and electric elements
- Medium-fast tempo (120-140 BPM)
- Suitable for group singing and community events
- Sense of freedom and adventure

**詳細プロンプト：**
```
Create an upbeat, adventurous Japanese pop-rock song with an energetic and uplifting atmosphere. The song should have a catchy, memorable chorus that people can sing along to. Include elements of freedom, adventure, and camaraderie. Medium-fast tempo (120-140 BPM), modern production with acoustic and electric instruments. The melody should convey a sense of excitement about traveling by propeller plane and exploring islands. Suitable for a community event theme song. Include a sense of breaking boundaries and challenging conventions.
```

```
Create a joyful, upbeat Disney-style parade song similar to "Harmony in Color". The song should be:
- Extremely catchy and danceable with infectious rhythm
- Rich with layered group choruses throughout (call-and-response style)
- Includes hand claps (パン、パン、パパパン) as rhythmic elements
- Bright, cheerful, and exciting atmosphere that makes you want to dance
- Pop-orchestral arrangement with brass, strings, and percussion
- Medium-fast tempo (130-145 BPM)
- Multiple vocal layers with group harmonies
- Suitable for a parade or celebration theme song
- Makes you want to clap along and sing together
- Energetic and uplifting, full of wonder and excitement
```

## 推奨設定

- **スタイル**: Japanese pop-rock, J-pop, upbeat, adventurous
- **雰囲気**: Energetic, uplifting, inspiring, adventurous, free-spirited
- **楽器**: Electric guitar, acoustic guitar, drums, bass, synthesizer, maybe some orchestral elements
- **テンポ**: Medium-fast (120-140 BPM)
- **ボーカル**: Energetic male/female voice, group chorus for the hook
- **長さ**: 3-4 minutes (full version), 1-2 minutes (short version)
- **効果音**: プロペラ機の音（オプション）

### Harmony in Color風の追加設定

- **スタイル**: Disney parade song style, pop-orchestral, joyful and danceable
- **雰囲気**: Bright, cheerful, exciting, makes you want to dance and clap along
- **楽器**: Brass section, strings, percussion, hand claps, layered vocals
- **テンポ**: Medium-fast (130-145 BPM)
- **ボーカル**: Multiple vocal layers, group harmonies, call-and-response style
- **コーラス**: Rich layered choruses throughout the entire song
- **リズム**: Hand claps (パン、パン、パパパン) as rhythmic elements
- **長さ**: 3-4 minutes (full version with extended choruses)
